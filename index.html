    About Me

    Hello there! I am a professional longboard skater who has spent the past several years coaching and leading free workshops in the Los Angeles region. As a product development enthusiast, I have employed my prototyping and design skills to assist my sponsors in successfully penetrating the longboard market. My endeavors have been featured in promotional videos and I have competed in both The Netherlands and France.

    Following a three-month backpacking trip across Turkey and Europe, I discovered a passion for web development. The limitless potential for creativity and design achievable through coding is remarkable. With a background in photography and music performance, I find that Front End Development is a natural extension of my artistic outlets. My proficiency in software development has bolstered my critical and analytical skills, and I am eagerly anticipating my next project.
